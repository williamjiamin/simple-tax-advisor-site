About

Simple税务师 is an independent local-first study app for 税务师职业资格考试 learners. It is not official, not endorsed, and not authorized by any examination authority, certification body, government agency, or official 税务师 program.

Important Notes

  • The app is a study aid and cannot guarantee exam results.
  • The app contains original/illustrative practice content, not real exam questions.
  • The continuing education feature is for personal self-study tracking only.
  • The exam checklist is a personal planning aid and does not submit or confirm registration.
  • Progress, bookmarks, attempts, wrong answers, favorites, study notes, exam checklist state, and self-study entries are stored locally on the device.

Troubleshooting

If the app behaves unexpectedly, restart the device and confirm you are using the latest App Store version. To remove all local app data, delete the app from the device.
